Blingee.com

Blingee is a website that anyone can use to make little animated PNGs, adding cosmetic touches like glitter text or animated confetti. It was very
popular with tweens around the MySpace era, but has since seen a return to popularity among young adults today.

Homestarrunner.com

The pinnacle of 2000s flash animated content, Homestar Runner made waves in cyberspace when it first debuted in the year 2000. It was, and still is,
loved by many for its sharp wit and iconic characters, like the eponymous Homestar Runner himself.

When software company Adobe announced they were shutting off support for Flash in all browsers at the end of 2020, many worried that
it would be the end of this iconic website. However, with a little technological know-how and a lot of elbow grease, the website managed to see it through to this very day.
